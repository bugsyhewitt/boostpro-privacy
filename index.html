Privacy Policy

BoostPro EQ and Volume

Last updated: July 2, 2026

BoostPro collects no data. None.

BoostPro EQ and Volume ("BoostPro", "the app", "we") is designed to work entirely on your device. We built it specifically to avoid the data collection common in audio-enhancement apps. This policy explains, plainly, what the app does and does not do with your information.

Information we collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. BoostPro has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no advertising. The app makes no network calls for its core function; your audio settings and presets never leave your phone.

What stays on your device

Your equalizer curves, saved presets, and app preferences (such as your chosen theme and whether to start on boot) are stored locally on your device using Android's standard on-device storage. This data is not transmitted anywhere. Uninstalling the app removes it.

Microphone and audio

BoostPro never accesses your microphone. It does not request the RECORD_AUDIO permission and does not record, capture, or listen to any audio. It applies an audio effect to your device's output using Android's system audio-effect API — it does not read the audio content itself.

Permissions we use, and why

  • Modify audio settings — to attach the equalizer and limiter to your device's audio output.
  • Foreground service — to keep the effect running and provide the quick-toggle notification.
  • Post notifications — to show that quick-toggle notification.
  • Receive boot completed — only if you enable "Start on boot", to restore your effect after a restart.

We do not request location, contacts, phone, camera, microphone, or broad storage access.

Third parties

BoostPro contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. An optional "Donate" button, if you choose to use it, may open an external payment page in your browser; any information you provide there is handled by that payment provider under its own privacy policy, not by BoostPro.

Children's privacy

BoostPro does not collect data from anyone, including children, and contains no content directed at or inappropriate for children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we will update the date above and post the revised version at this URL.
